About the Summit
The new immersive experience entertainment format represented by the Chamber of Secrets, Script Killing, Immersed Drama, New Media Exhibition and XR is changing commercial real estate and cultural tourism real estate, and then affecting all existing business models.
With its global influence in the industry and extensive contacts at home and abroad, NeXT SCENE held the first global immersive experience entertainment industry summit in Shanghai on November 10-11, 2021. The summit will focus on China's local development and international dialogue, promote the development of immersive experiential entertainment and establish a sustainable cooperative network ecology by establishing an international cooperation alliance.
